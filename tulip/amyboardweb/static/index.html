Synthesizer

AMY, supports up to 120 oscillators, stereo sound, filters, reverb, chorus, FM, PCM samples (baked in or loaded from a file), and comes with a Python library for managing voices and patches for polyphony and multitimbral operation. You can write all your patches, music or interactions with other synths in pure Python.

Getting Started on a XX

Write your music in Python. See more

 
# Play a random note of an F minor 7 chord. 
# Beat syncs with other running music programs
import synth, music, random, sequencer

chord = music.Chord("F:min7").midinotes()
synth = synth.PatchSynth(1) # single note polyphony
synth.program_change(143) # DX7 BASS 2 patch

# Play random note every quarter note
seq = sequencer.Sequence(4, 8) 
for i in range(8):
    seq.add(i, syn.note_on, [random.choice(chord), 0.6])
