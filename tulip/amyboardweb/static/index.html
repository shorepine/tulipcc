What's an AMYboard?

AMYboard is a complete synthesizer module with jacks and a front panel powered by AMY. You can use it standalone, or as a Eurorack module (10HP), or with your Tulip or as a dev board for your own DIY synth. It's cheap, and open source, and can be programmed over the web. You can write your music and synth setup in code, control it with CV or MIDI, and add your own accessories like knobs or a screen. Join our fun community and make some great music.
