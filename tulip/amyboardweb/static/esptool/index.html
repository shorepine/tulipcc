AMYboard Upgrade
  • Connect your AMYboard to your device over USB.
  • Hold down both buttons on the side, then let go of RST, then let go of BOOT.
    See button locations:
  • Click the Search for AMYboard button below and choose the device that says USB/JTAG or AMYboard when the window pops up, then Connect
  • Choose to either:
    • Upgrade AMYboard firmware. Your stored data should be safe.
    • Fully erase and re-flash AMYboard. This will remove any stored patches or environments. (Your SD card is safe.) Use this only if you're having issues.
  • Wait a minute until the progress bar disappears. Hit RST. Your AMYboard should be updated!
